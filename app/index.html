100% ECO friendly long before it became a buzzword

Environmentalism has been our mandate since the very beginning of Saraya’s existence and long before "eco-friendliness" became a buzzword. We are ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certified for quality control and environmental management systems.

We offer a wide range of biodegradable, natural products. We also invest in green technologies, conservation initiatives and waste reduction programs across all our operations.
