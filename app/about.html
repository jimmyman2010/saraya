Blue Label

The world’s leading distributor and marketer of witch hazel based products

Dickinson Brands is the world’s leading distributor and marketer of witch hazel based products for skin and personal care. Established in 1997, Dickinson Brands brought together the two heritage hallmarks of Dickinson’s and T.N. Dickinson’s which were established in the early 1800’s. Today, Dickinson Brands continues to expand on this foundation by bringing to market high quality, value-added products that capitalize on the natural healing properties of witch hazel.

Our Mission

Only the highest quality natural products

We at Dickinson Brands are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality natural products. We only use the finest ingredients and good manufacturing processes.

Our corporate offices are located in East Hampton, Connecticut, where all of our products are manufactured to the highest standards in a state-of-the-art facility.

Dickinson Brands markets our products in several countries throughout the world.
